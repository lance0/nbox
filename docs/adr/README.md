# Architecture Decision Records

Records of significant architectural decisions made during nbox development.
Each record captures the context, decision, and consequences so future
contributors understand why the design exists, not just what changed.

|-----|-------|--------|------|

## Template

New ADRs should follow this format:

```markdown
# ADR-NNNN: Title

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXXX
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the issue or question being addressed?

## Decision

What was decided?

## Consequences

What are the results - positive, negative, and neutral?

## References

Sources that informed the decision (optional).
```
